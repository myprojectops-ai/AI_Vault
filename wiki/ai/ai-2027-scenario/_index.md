# AI 2027 Scenario

The AI 2027 project is a research-backed scenario forecast published April 2025 by
Daniel Kokotajlo, Eli Lifland, Thomas Larsen, and Romeo Dean (with writing help from
Scott Alexander). It depicts a concrete, quantitative timeline for how superhuman AI
might unfold from mid-2025 through late 2027.
