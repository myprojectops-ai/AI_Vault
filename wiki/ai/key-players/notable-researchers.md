# Notable AI Researchers and Figures

Key individuals shaping the development, safety, and governance of AI.

## AI 2027 Authors

### Daniel Kokotajlo
- Former OpenAI researcher; left over safety concerns
- Founded AI Futures Project
- Named to TIME100 list
- Published "What 2026 Looks Like" (2021) which predicted chain-of-thought,
  inference scaling, and chip export controls before ChatGPT existed
- Primary architect of the AI 2027 scenario

### Eli Lifland
- Co-founded AI Digest
- Ranks #1 on RAND Forecasting Initiative all-time leaderboard
- AI robustness researcher

### Scott Alexander
- Blogger (Slate Star Codex / Astral Codex Ten)
- Rewrote AI 2027 in engaging prose style
- Influential voice in rationalist and AI safety communities

## Lab Leaders

### Sam Altman (OpenAI CEO)
- Predicted AGI within 5 years; targeting "superintelligence in the true sense"
- Central figure in the AI acceleration narrative
- Has stated OpenAI is "setting its sights on the glorious future"

### Dario Amodei (Anthropic CEO)
- Former OpenAI VP of Research; left to found Anthropic
- Published "Machines of Loving Grace" essay on positive AI outcomes
- Coined "country of geniuses in a datacenter" (referenced in AI 2027)
- Advocates for "race to the top" on safety

### Demis Hassabis (Google DeepMind CEO)
- Nobel Prize 2024 (for AlphaFold protein structure prediction)
- Predicted AGI within 5 years
- Leads the merged Google Brain + DeepMind organization

### Yann LeCun (Meta AI, Chief AI Scientist)
- Turing Award winner
- Vocal skeptic of AGI timelines and existential risk framing
- Champions open-source AI development
- Argues current approaches won't reach AGI

## Safety and Alignment Researchers

### Yoshua Bengio
- Turing Award winner (deep learning pioneer)
- Endorsed AI 2027 as valuable for identifying important questions
- Signed open letters calling for AI development pause
- Increasingly vocal about existential risk from AI

### Jan Leike
- Former OpenAI alignment team lead; departed 2024 over safety concerns
- Joined Anthropic
- Co-authored the "superalignment" plan at OpenAI with Ilya Sutskever

### Ilya Sutskever
- OpenAI co-founder and former Chief Scientist
- Departed 2024; co-founded Safe Superintelligence Inc. (SSI)
- Famous for "feeling the AGI" moment
- SSI focused exclusively on building safe superintelligence

## Key Takeaways

- The AI leadership landscape spans accelerationists (Altman), cautious optimists
  (Amodei), skeptics (LeCun), and safety advocates (Bengio, Sutskever)
- Many key safety researchers have left frontier labs over concerns
- The AI 2027 authors have strong forecasting track records
- The debate between "race ahead" and "slow down" plays out among these individuals
